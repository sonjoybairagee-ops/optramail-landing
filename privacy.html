Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to OptraMail. Your privacy is our top priority. We believe in being fully transparent about the data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. What Data Do We Collect?

OptraMail is designed exclusively to track email performance and manage your CRM. We do NOT read your personal emails or store your passwords. We only collect:

  • Email Tracking Data: Information regarding whether your sent emails were opened, when they were opened, and if links or PDFs within the email were clicked or downloaded.
  • CRM Data: The names, emails, and company details of the leads you explicitly add to your OptraMail CRM.
  • Basic Account Information: Your email address, used strictly to authenticate you via the extension and map your tracking data.

2. How Do We Use Your Data?

The information we collect is used entirely to power the features of the OptraMail extension:

  • To generate your "My Performance" dashboard and Engagement Scores.
  • To provide you with a real-time timeline of opens and clicks.
  • To allow you to manage your sales pipeline efficiently.

3. Data Protection and Storage

All data is securely transmitted over HTTPS and stored securely. We do not sell, rent, or share your tracking data or CRM leads with any third parties.

4. Extension Permissions

The OptraMail Chrome Extension requires specific permissions to function:

  • Storage: Used to temporarily store your tracking mode preferences and authentication state.
  • Host Permissions: Required to inject the tracking pixel and wrapper links into the emails you choose to send via Gmail.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the security of your data, please contact us directly at support@optramail.com.
